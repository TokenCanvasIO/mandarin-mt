# Michel Thomas alignment + roadmap to Advanced

## Consolidated audit (my read + Grok, converged 2026)
- METHOD FIDELITY: faithful. Student constructs from English, builds from known blocks,
  two virtual students + user as third, thinking-time pauses, no-stress redirects,
  functional-first ordering (numbers delayed), unfolding into long sentences. One deliberate
  deviation: real MT is audio-only; this shows pinyin/characters AFTER audio (needed for the
  tone-colour system).
- LEVEL: Foundation-complete (~90-95% of Goodman's Foundation). ZERO Intermediate grammar,
  ZERO Advanced grammar. Current ceiling ~ lower-intermediate descriptive ability.
- NOT "beginner to advanced" yet. ~14 more lessons needed (Intermediate 6-8 + Advanced 4-6
  + vocabulary 2-3).

## Quick Foundation polish (small, do first)
- Show 她 as a distinct character alongside 他 (same sound tā).
- Add 请 qǐng (please) and bring 跟 gēn (with) in earlier, not just the last drill.
- Optional: numbers past 12 (tens/hundreds), a couple more measure words.

## Intermediate band (build next — ranked, 了 first)
1. 了 le — completion / change of state  (HIGHEST VALUE, the intermediate pivot)
2. 比 bǐ / 更 gèng / 最 zuì — comparatives
3. Resultative complements — 看见, 听懂, 做完, 找到
4. Question words — 什么, 哪儿, 怎么, 为什么, 多少
5. 给 gěi (give/for), 到 dào (arrive) vs 在 zài
6. 过 guo (experiential), 着 zhe (ongoing state)
7. Directional complements — 进来, 出去, 回来
8. 应该 yīnggāi, 可能 kěnéng; 让 ràng; more measure words; time-duration; 要…了

## Advanced band (after Intermediate)
把 bǎ, 被 bèi, 是…的, potential complements (看得懂 / 吃不完), complex directional chains,
虽然…但是 / 如果…就 / 连…都 / 除了…以外 / 越…越, 的 relative clauses (我买的书), 不但…而且.

## Approach
Same engine, same tone-colour + sandhi + gesture system, same audio pipeline (each new lesson's
phrases get pre-generated into audio/ by scripts/gen-audio.py). New lessons slot in as Lesson 10+.
