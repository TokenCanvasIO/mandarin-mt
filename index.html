Pinyin is spelled differently from English. Learn these, then the words are easy. Tap any example to hear Jingtao.
The ü sound
Say "oo" with your lips pushed forward, but put your tongue where it goes for "ee". No English equivalent. After j, q, x, y it is written as a plain u but it is always ü.
